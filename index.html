People get distracted and then they might die or kill other people in the accident.They can also damage the car or wreck anything around them. This makes a serious problem that we know we can solve.              Our solution is that they have to get the SafeWheel steering wheel cover. This will prevent the urge to look at your phone while you’re driving.          The thing about this product that makes it different is that it does not just diagnose the problem, it solves it. This was the first error we ran into, but we solved it by using touch sensors. Now, whenever you touch it, it will automatically lock your phone making impossible to use until you remove pressure from the wheel.
